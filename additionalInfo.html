Clinic Appointment Protocols and Provider Assignment

This document outlines the standard operating procedures for appointment scheduling, time management, and provider-specific guidelines. Adherence to these protocols ensures efficient clinic flow and optimal patient care.

Appointment Duration Protocols

Appointments must be scheduled according to the following time allocations to ensure comprehensive care:

  • 15-Minute Visits: Reserved strictly for **Sick Visits**.
  • 30-Minute Visits: Required for all complex, new, or procedural appointments, including:
    • New Patients (Initial Consultations)
    • Well Child Visits, Adwell Visits, and All Physicals (including DOT)
    • Hospital Follow-up (F/U)
    • All Procedures (e.g., Sutures/Removal, Paps)
    • Nexplanon removal or placement

Appointment Slot Management Policy

Efficient use of the scheduling template is mandatory:

  • **30-Minute Slots:** Must be fully utilized.
  • **Scheduling Template:** Prioritize filling pre-defined slots. If a 30-minute slot is necessary but unavailable, **consult with the rendering provider** before converting a 15-minute slot.
  • **Slot Conversion (Last Resort):** A 30-minute slot may be split into two 15-minute appointments only as a **last resort** or within **72 hours** of the scheduled time.
  • **Same Day / Walk-In:** Use designated **SD/Walk-in slots**. Patients present in the clinic may be checked in immediately.
